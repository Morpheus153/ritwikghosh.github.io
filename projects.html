Some projects that I made

Destiny: choice based game app Flutter

A game with a story line that is determined by the choices of the user. There are multiple storylines and the user's decision influences the outcome of the game. It is based on Flutter and can be used on both iOS and Android devices.


Quiz App Flutter

A quiz where the user gets to see their live scores as the player answers and moves to the next question. It is based on Flutter and can be used on both iOS and Android devices.


Xylophone App Flutter

An aesthetic looking and sounding Xylophone right in the comfort of your pocket. It is based on Flutter and can be used on both iOS and Android devices.


Magic-8-Ball App Flutter

A magic ball that answers some of your important questions in life and helps you make decisions. Not really though. It is based on Flutter and can be used on both iOS and Android devices.


Dice App Flutter

A Flutter mobile app for both iOS and Android that lets you roll a pair of dice.


Business Card App Flutter

A flutter app that serves as a digital business card, complete with a QR code to my website. It is based on Flutter and can be used on both iOS and Android devices.
